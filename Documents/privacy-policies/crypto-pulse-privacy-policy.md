# Privacy Policy for Crypto Pulse

**Last Updated:** April 21, 2026

## Overview

Crypto Pulse is a Chrome extension that provides real-time cryptocurrency price movement alerts. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Selected currencies** (BTC, ETH, etc.) - stored locally in your browser
- **Sensitivity settings** (High/Medium/Low) - stored locally in your browser
- **Notification preferences** - stored locally in your browser

### Automatically Collected Data
- **Price history** of tracked cryptocurrencies - stored locally in your browser
- **Alert history** (timestamps, price changes) - stored locally in your browser
- **Theme preference** (light/dark mode) - stored locally in your browser
- **Language preference** (EN/RU) - stored locally in your browser

## How We Use Your Data

All data is used exclusively for:
- Monitoring cryptocurrency price movements based on your selected settings
- Sending desktop notifications when price thresholds are met
- Displaying price charts and alert history within the extension
- Remembering your preferences between browser sessions

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's `storage.local` API.** 

We do NOT:
- Send any data to external servers
- Collect personal identifiable information
- Track your browsing history
- Share any information with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking cookies
- Store data in the cloud

## Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by:
1. Right-clicking the Crypto Pulse icon in Chrome
2. Selecting "Remove from Chrome"
3. Or manually clearing extension data in `chrome://extensions/`

## Third-Party Services

Crypto Pulse fetches real-time cryptocurrency prices from:
- **Binance API** (`https://api.binance.com`)

We only fetch public price data. No personal information is sent to Binance.

## Notifications

The extension requests notification permission only to send you price alerts. You can disable notifications at any time:
- Through the extension toggle switch
- Or in Chrome settings: `chrome://settings/content/notifications`
