# Privacy Policy for RetroDeck

**Last updated:** May 1, 2026

## Overview
RetroDeck is a browser extension that provides offline retro games. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data Collection

### What we DO NOT collect:
- No personal information (name, email, address, phone)
- No browsing history
- No keystrokes or inputs from non-game interactions
- No location data
- No analytics or tracking pixels
- No crash reports automatically sent

### What we DO collect (stored locally only):
- **Game scores** — stored in your browser's local storage to show your best results
- **Theme preference** — stored locally to remember your light/dark mode choice

All data is stored **exclusively on your device** using Chrome's `storage.local` API. No data is transmitted to any server.

## Third-Party Services
RetroDeck does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external APIs. All games run completely offline.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or transfer any data to third parties. Since we don't collect personal data, there is nothing to share.

## Your Rights
You can clear all stored data at any time by:
1. Right-click the extension icon → "Inspect popup"
2. Go to Application → Local Storage → Clear
3. Or simply uninstall the extension

## Children's Privacy
RetroDeck does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.
